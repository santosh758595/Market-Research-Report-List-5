<p><p>Ski helmets are generally available in two main types: PC shell and ABS shell. PC shell helmets are made from polycarbonate material, which is lightweight and offers good impact resistance. These helmets are often preferred for their sleek design and durability. On the other hand, ABS shell helmets are constructed from acrylonitrile butadiene styrene material, offering enhanced durability and impact protection. They are slightly heavier than PC shell helmets but provide excellent protection in more extreme skiing conditions. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of skiers.</p></p>
