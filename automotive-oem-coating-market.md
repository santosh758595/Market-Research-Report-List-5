<p><p>Automotive OEM coatings are categorized into four main types - solvent-borne, waterborne, powdered, and others. Solvent-borne coatings use volatile organic compounds as a solvent, waterborne coatings contain water as a primary solvent, and powdered coatings are applied in a dry form and then heated to form a protective layer. Other types of coatings may include UV-cured or high-solid coatings. Each type has its advantages and is used based on factors such as environmental regulations, performance requirements, and cost considerations in the automotive industry.</p></p>
