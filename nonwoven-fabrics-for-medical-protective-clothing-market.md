<p><p>Nonwoven fabrics for medical protective clothing are available in various types such as SMS nonwoven, SMMS nonwoven, SMMMS nonwoven, and other markets. SMS nonwoven is made of spunbond and meltblown layers, providing good barrier properties. SMMS nonwoven has an additional spunbond layer for increased strength and barrier protection. SMMMS nonwoven consists of multiple layers of spunbond and meltblown fabrics, offering superior protection. Other markets in this category may include nonwoven fabrics with unique combinations of layers for specific medical applications.</p></p>
