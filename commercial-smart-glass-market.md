<p><p>The commercial smart glass market includes three main types: Electrochromic glass, Thermochromic glass, and Photochromic glass. Electrochromic glass offers controllable tinting through an applied voltage, providing privacy and energy efficiency. Thermochromic glass changes tint based on temperature fluctuations, offering passive climate control. Photochromic glass darkens in response to UV light exposure, providing protection from harsh sunlight. Each type of smart glass offers unique benefits for commercial applications, catering to varying needs for privacy, energy efficiency, and comfort.</p></p>
