<p><p>Nucleic Acid Amplification Testing (NAAT) market types include Target Amplification Systems, Probe Amplification Systems, and Signal Amplification. Target Amplification Systems amplify specific regions of nucleic acids through techniques like Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR). Probe Amplification Systems detect and amplify target nucleic acids using labeled probes. Signal Amplification methods increase the detection sensitivity by amplifying the signal generated by target nucleic acids. These different types of NAAT technologies play a crucial role in diagnosing infectious diseases and genetic disorders with high accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
