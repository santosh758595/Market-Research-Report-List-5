<p><p>Polyethylene Terephthalate Containers market consists of three main types: Stretch Blow Molding, Extrusion Blow Molding, and Thermoforming. Stretch Blow Molding involves stretching a preform before blowing it into the desired shape. Extrusion Blow Molding involves extruding a parison and then blowing it into the mold. Thermoforming uses heat to shape the PET sheet into the desired container shape. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in various industries for packaging solutions.</p></p>
