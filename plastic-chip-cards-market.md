<p><p>Plastic chip cards are divided into two main market types: contact cards and contactless cards. Contact cards require physical contact with a card reader for data transmission, while contactless cards use radio-frequency identification technology for wireless communication. Contact cards are commonly used in applications like debit and credit cards, ID cards, and access control. Contactless cards are gaining popularity for their convenience and speed in payment transactions, transportation systems, and security access.
