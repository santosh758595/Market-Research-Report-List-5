<p><p>Knee reconstruction market types include cemented implants, cementless implants, partial implants, and revision implants. Cemented implants involve using bone cement to hold the implant in place, while cementless implants rely on the bone growing into the implant for stability. Partial implants are used when only part of the knee joint needs to be replaced, and revision implants are used to replace a failed previous implant. Each type of implant offers different benefits and is chosen based on the patient's specific needs and circumstances.</p></p>
