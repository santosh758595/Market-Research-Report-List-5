<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Double Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Room hygrostats market can be divided into two main types: single stage and double stage. In a single stage market, the hygrostat operates at one set point, turning on or off the humidifier or dehumidifier based on the current humidity level. In a double stage market, the hygrostat operates at two set points, allowing for more precise control over the humidity levels. Double stage hygrostats are often more advanced and offer better accuracy in maintaining the desired humidity levels in a room.</p></p>
