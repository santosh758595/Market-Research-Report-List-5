<p><p>Mobile PMIC market types include CMOS, BiCMOS, and BCD. CMOS technology is popular for its low power consumption and integration capabilities. BiCMOS combines the advantages of both bipolar and CMOS technologies, offering high-speed performance and low power consumption. BCD technology is known for its ability to handle high voltage levels efficiently. Each type caters to different requirements in the mobile PMIC market, offering a range of features such as power efficiency, speed, and voltage handling to meet the diverse needs of mobile devices.</p></p>
