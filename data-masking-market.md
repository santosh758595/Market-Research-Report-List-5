<p><p>Data masking market can be divided into two main types: static and dynamic. </p><p>Static data masking involves the permanent transformation of sensitive data before it is stored or transmitted, providing a consistent level of protection. On the other hand, dynamic data masking involves the real-time obfuscation of sensitive data during access, allowing organizations to control who can view the original data without permanently altering it.</p><p>Both static and dynamic data masking solutions play a critical role in safeguarding sensitive information and ensuring compliance with data privacy regulations.</p></p>
