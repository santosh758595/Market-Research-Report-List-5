<p><p>Robotic-assisted imaging technologies have various types within the market based on different imaging modalities. These include X-Ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound, and others. X-Ray imaging modalities use electromagnetic radiation to create images of the inside of the body. CT scans use multiple X-rays to produce detailed cross-sectional images. MRI scans use magnetic fields and radio waves to create detailed images of organs and tissues. Ultrasound imaging uses sound waves to create real-time images. Other modalities may include PET scans, SPECT scans, and nuclear medicine imaging.</p></p>
