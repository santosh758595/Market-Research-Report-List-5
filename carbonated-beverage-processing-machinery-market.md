Blenders & mixers are used to mix different ingredients. Heat exchangers help in heating or cooling the liquid. Silos store ingredients or finished products. Filtration equipment is used to remove impurities from the liquid before bottling. These equipment play a crucial role in the production process of carbonated beverages.</p></p>
