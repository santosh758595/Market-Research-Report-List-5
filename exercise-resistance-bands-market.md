<p><p>Exercise resistance bands come in two main types: strip bands and tube bands. Strip bands are flat and usually have handles on each end, making them ideal for exercises like bicep curls or shoulder presses. Tube bands, on the other hand, are cylindrical with handles on each end, allowing for a wider range of motion and versatility in exercises like squats or rows. Both types offer varying levels of resistance to help users customize their workouts and improve strength and flexibility.</p></p>
