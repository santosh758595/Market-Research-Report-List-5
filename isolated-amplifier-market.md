<p><p>Isolated amplifiers are classified based on the method used to achieve isolation between input and output signals. Optoelectronic isolated amplifiers use light to transfer signals, ensuring high levels of isolation. Capacitor isolated amplifiers use capacitors to transmit signals, providing effective isolation. Transformer isolated amplifiers utilize transformers to transfer signals, offering reliable isolation. Each type of isolated amplifier offers unique features and benefits, catering to different application requirements in industries such as medical, industrial, and automotive.</p></p>
