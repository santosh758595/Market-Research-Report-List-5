<p><p>Textile machines are categorized into various types based on their functions. Spinning machines are used to convert raw materials like cotton, wool, or synthetic fibers into yarn. Weaving machines are utilized to interlace two sets of yarns at right angles to create fabric. Knitting machines are designed to create knitted fabrics or garments by interlocking yarns. Texturing machines are used to add texture or special effects to fabrics. Each of these market types plays a crucial role in the textile industry.</p></p>
