These sensors are divided into two main market types: LIN (Local Interconnect Network) and CAN (Controller Area Network). LIN sensors are typically used in smaller, simpler battery systems, while CAN sensors are more commonly found in larger, more complex battery systems. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of the battery module in vehicles.</p></p>
