<p><p>Satellite Earth Observation Market includes three types: Low Earth Orbit (LEO), Medium Earth Orbit (MEO), and Geosynchronous Earth Orbit (GEO). LEO satellites orbit closest to Earth, providing high-resolution imaging for applications like disaster monitoring. MEO satellites are positioned further out, offering wider coverage for services such as navigation systems. GEO satellites remain stationary over a specific point on Earth, enabling continuous monitoring for applications like weather forecasting. Each market type serves different purposes based on their respective orbits and capabilities.</p></p>
