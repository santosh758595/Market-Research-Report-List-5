<p><p>Automatic Pipetting Workstations are categorized into two types: Single-channel Automated Pipetting Workstations and Multi-channel Automated Pipetting Workstations. Both types cater to the diverse needs of laboratories and research facilities, offering efficiency and reliability in liquid handling processes.</p></p>
