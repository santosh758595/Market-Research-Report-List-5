<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Type</li><li>Piezoresistive Effect Type</li><li>Resistance - Strain Effect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Combustion Pressure Sensor market consists of three main types: Electromagnetic Type, Piezoresistive Effect Type, and Resistance-Strain Effect Type. The Electromagnetic Type sensors use the principle of electromagnetic induction to measure pressure, while Piezoresistive Effect Type sensors utilize the change in resistance of a material under pressure. Resistance-Strain Effect Type sensors measure pressure by detecting the strain on a material. Each type of sensor has its own advantages and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
