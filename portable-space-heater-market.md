<p><p>Portable space heaters come in different types to suit various heating needs. Convector heaters work by drawing cold air over a heating element which then warms up the air before circulating it back into the room. Radiant heaters, on the other hand, emit infrared radiation to directly heat objects and people in their path. Both convector and radiant heaters are popular in the market due to their portability and efficient heating capabilities. Customers can choose the type of heater based on their preference for fast or focused heating.</p></p>
