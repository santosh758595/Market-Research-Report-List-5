<p><p>Aircraft door dampers can be categorized into three main types based on their design and function: rotary aircraft dampers, linear aircraft dampers, and axial aircraft dampers. Rotary dampers provide resistance to rotational movement, linear dampers offer resistance to linear motion, and axial dampers control movement along the axis. Each type of damper is specifically designed to effectively control the closing and opening of aircraft doors, ensuring smooth operation and enhancing passenger safety during flight.</p></p>
