<p><strong>The Steel Coupling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Coupling</li><li>Flexible Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel coupling market can be classified into two main types: rigid coupling and flexible coupling. Rigid couplings are used in applications where precise shaft alignment is needed, providing a strong and stable connection between shafts. On the other hand, flexible couplings offer more flexibility and can accommodate misalignment, vibration, and shock loads. They are commonly used in applications where there is a need for some degree of movement between shafts. Both types of couplings play a crucial role in connecting shafts in various industrial applications.</p></p>
