<p><p>The hydraulic components market for construction machinery can be divided into four main types: power elements, actuators, control elements, and auxiliary components. Power elements include hydraulic pumps and motors that generate hydraulic power. Actuators, such as cylinders and hydraulic motors, convert the hydraulic power into mechanical energy. Control elements, like valves and hydraulic fluids, regulate the flow and pressure of the hydraulic system. Auxiliary components, such as filters and accumulators, support the overall functionality of the hydraulic system in construction machinery.</p></p>
