<p><ul><li>Needle Coke</li><li>Shot Coke</li><li>Sponge Coke</li><li>Honeycomb Coke</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Calcined Petroleum Coke market includes four main types: Needle Coke, Shot Coke, Sponge Coke, and Honeycomb Coke. Needle Coke is a high-quality coke used in the production of graphite electrodes. Shot Coke is a byproduct of the delayed coking process and is often used as a solid fuel. Sponge Coke has high porosity and is used in the production of anodes for aluminum smelting. Honeycomb Coke has a honeycomb-like structure and is primarily used in the production of carbon anodes for the aluminum industry.</p></p>
