<p><p>Soundproof mineral wool is categorized into four types based on their form and application: Felty, Strip, Tubular, and Granular. Felty mineral wool is fluffy and resembles a felt material, ideal for filling gaps and cracks. Strip mineral wool comes in thin strips for easy installation in walls and ceilings. Tubular mineral wool is cylindrical in shape and used for insulating pipes and ducts. Granular mineral wool is small particles used for filling cavities and attics to provide soundproofing and insulation.</p></p>
