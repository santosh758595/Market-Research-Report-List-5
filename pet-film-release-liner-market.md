<p><p>PET Film Release Liner market can be classified into two main types: Silicone Film and Non-Silicone Film. Silicone Film release liners have a coating of silicone on one side, which provides a smooth and non-sticky surface for easy release of adhesive materials. On the other hand, Non-Silicone Film release liners do not contain silicone coating and are often used in applications where silicone residue is not desirable. Both types of release liners cater to different industry needs and preferences.</p></p>
