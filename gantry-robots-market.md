<p><p>Gantry robots are classified into two main types: open gantry robots and closed gantry robots. Open gantry robots have an open structure, allowing for easy access and visibility of the work area. They are commonly used in industries where flexibility and customization are key. On the other hand, closed gantry robots have a closed structure, providing protection for the internal components. They are often used in industries that require precision, cleanliness, and protection against environmental factors.</p></p>
