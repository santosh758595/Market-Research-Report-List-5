<p><p>Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) Systems are used for real-time temperature monitoring in various industries. There are two main types of DTS systems based on the type of optical fiber used: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode. Single-Mode DTS systems use a single light beam to transmit data along the fiber, providing higher resolution and longer sensing distances. Multi-Mode DTS systems use multiple light beams, offering lower resolution but at a lower cost. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications.</p></p>
