<p><p>Moisture Cure Adhesive market is divided into two main types: Rigid Moisture Cure Adhesive and Soft Moisture Cure Adhesive. Rigid Moisture Cure Adhesives offer strong bonding properties and are commonly used in applications where a hard, durable bond is required. On the other hand, Soft Moisture Cure Adhesives are more flexible and offer some level of elasticity, making them suitable for applications where movement or vibration may occur. Both types of adhesives cure through exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient and versatile for various bonding needs.</p></p>
