<p><p>Oilseed extraction equipment market includes two main types: Conventional Extraction and Ultrasonic Extraction. Conventional Extraction involves using traditional methods like solvent extraction or mechanical pressing to extract oil from oilseeds. On the other hand, Ultrasonic Extraction uses ultrasonic waves to break cell walls of oilseeds, resulting in higher oil yields in a shorter time. Both types of extraction methods are utilized in the oilseed industry, with Ultrasonic Extraction gaining popularity due to its efficiency and sustainability benefits.</p></p>
