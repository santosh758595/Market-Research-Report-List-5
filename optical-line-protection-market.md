<p><p>Optical Line Protection is a technology used in telecommunications networks to provide redundancy and ensure uninterrupted data transmission. The 1+1 Optical Line Protection market offers a primary and a secondary path for data transmission, with the secondary path automatically taking over in case of a failure in the primary path. On the other hand, the 1:1 Optical Line Protection market provides a dedicated backup path for each main path, offering even greater reliability and fault tolerance. Both types of protection help ensure high availability and reliability in network operations.</p></p>
