<p><p>Weather-proof glass cement can be classified into two main types based on their chemical properties: acid-resistant and neutral. Acid-resistant glass cement is designed to withstand exposure to harsh acids and other corrosive chemicals, making it ideal for applications in industries such as chemical processing and automotive manufacturing. On the other hand, neutral glass cement is formulated to provide general weather resistance without a specific focus on acid resistance, making it suitable for more common applications such as construction and outdoor furniture.</p></p>
