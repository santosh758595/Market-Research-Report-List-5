Embedded antennas, on the other hand, are integrated directly into the UAV's structure, providing a more streamlined and permanent solution. Both types cater to different requirements and preferences of UAV operators, with portable antennas offering flexibility and convenience, while embedded antennas offer a more compact and integrated solution.</p></p>
