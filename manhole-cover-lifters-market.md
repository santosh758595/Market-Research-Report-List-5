<p><p>Manhole cover lifters come in two main types: wheel type and wheelless type. Wheel type lifters have wheels attached to the lifter, making it easier to move and position the lifter on the cover. This type is especially useful for covers that are heavier or harder to lift. On the other hand, wheelless type lifters do not have wheels but are designed for easier maneuverability and lifting of lighter covers. Both types offer convenient solutions for safely and efficiently accessing manholes.</p></p>
