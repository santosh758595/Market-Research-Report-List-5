<p><p>Solid-State Lighting Source Market Types consist of Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs), Organic Light Emitting Diodes (OLED), and Polymer Light Emitting Diodes (PLED). LEDs are semiconductor devices that emit light when an electric current passes through them. OLEDs use organic compounds to create light, providing more flexibility in design and potentially lower cost. PLEDs are similar to OLEDs, but use polymer materials instead of organic compounds. These technologies are revolutionizing the lighting industry with their energy efficiency, durability, and design flexibility.</p></p>
