<p><p>Deep-groove Ball Bearings are commonly classified into three main types based on the number of rows in the bearing: Single-Row Bearings, Double-Row Bearings, and Multi-Row Bearings. Single-Row Bearings consist of one row of balls, offering basic radial load capacity. Double-Row Bearings have two rows of balls, increasing radial load capacity and stiffness. Multi-Row Bearings, as the name suggests, have multiple rows of balls, providing even higher load capacity and rigidity, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
