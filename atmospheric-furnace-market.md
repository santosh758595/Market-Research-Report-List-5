<p><p>Atmospheric furnace market types can generally be classified into two categories: batch and continuous furnaces. Batch furnaces are designed for small-scale production and are used for processes that do not require continuous operation. They are ideal for heat treating, annealing, and tempering of small batches of materials. On the other hand, continuous furnaces are designed for large-scale production and operate continuously to process large volumes of materials. These furnaces are commonly used for processes such as baking, sintering, and brazing.</p></p>
