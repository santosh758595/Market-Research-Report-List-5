<p><ul><li>Addressable Connected Fire Detection Systems</li><li>Conventional Connected Fire Detection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connected fire detection systems have two main types in the market: Addressable Connected Fire Detection Systems and Conventional Connected Fire Detection Systems. Addressable systems have each device connected to a central control panel, allowing for specific identification of the location of a fire or fault. Conventional systems divide a building into zones with multiple devices connected to each zone. Both systems provide remote monitoring and control capabilities, enhancing overall safety and efficiency in fire detection and response.</p></p>
