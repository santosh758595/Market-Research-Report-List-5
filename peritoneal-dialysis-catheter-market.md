<p><p>Peritoneal Dialysis Catheters are medical devices used to perform peritoneal dialysis in patients with kidney failure. There are two main types of catheters used in this market: Straight Catheters and Pigtail-curled Catheters. Straight Catheters have a simple design with a straight tube that is inserted into the abdomen to allow for dialysis fluid exchange. Pigtail-curled Catheters have a curled end that helps prevent catheter dislodgement and provides better stability during dialysis sessions. Both types are essential in providing effective and safe peritoneal dialysis treatment.</p></p>
