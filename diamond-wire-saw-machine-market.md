<p><p>Diamond wire saw machines are classified into three types based on the type of diamond wire used - slurry wire, resin-based diamond wire, and long electroplated diamond wire. Slurry wire is typically used for cutting soft materials with high precision. Resin-based diamond wire is ideal for cutting hard materials due to its strong bond. Long electroplated diamond wire is suitable for cutting both hard and brittle materials with high efficiency. Each type of diamond wire saw machine caters to specific cutting needs in various industries.</p></p>
