<p><p>Gluten-free packaged food refers to food products that do not contain gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. Factors contributing to the market growth include the rising prevalence of celiac disease and gluten sensitivity, increased diagnosis rates, and the growing popularity of gluten-free diets among health-conscious consumers. Additionally, the expansion of distribution channels, improved product innovation, and marketing efforts by manufacturers are also driving market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the gluten-free packaged food market include the introduction of new gluten-free products, such as baking mixes, snacks, and ready-to-eat meals. Additionally, there is a growing demand for organic, non-GMO, and allergen-free gluten-free products among consumers.Overall, the gluten-free packaged food market is expected to continue to grow as more consumers seek out healthier and allergen-free food options.</p></p>
