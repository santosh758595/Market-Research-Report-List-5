Marine Drilling Risers are used for vertical movement of fluids during drilling operations in deep water environments. Tie-back Drilling Risers are used to connect existing subsea wells to new platforms or rigs for production purposes. Both types play a vital role in ensuring safe and efficient drilling operations in offshore environments.</p></p>
