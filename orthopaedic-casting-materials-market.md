<p><p>Orthopaedic casting materials market consists of three main types - plaster, fiberglass, and others. Plaster has been traditionally used for casting due to its low cost and ease of use, but it is heavy and less durable compared to fiberglass. Fiberglass offers better strength and durability, allowing for early weight-bearing. Other materials such as polyester and polyurethane are also used in some cases for casting. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the patient and the condition being treated.</p></p>
