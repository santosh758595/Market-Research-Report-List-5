<p><p>Wafer processing chemicals market includes various types such as sputtering targets, photoresists, wet-processing chemicals, CMP slurries, copper plating chemicals, and atmospheric and specialty gases. Wet-processing chemicals help in cleaning and etching wafers, while CMP slurries aid in planarizing surfaces. Copper plating chemicals are used for copper interconnects, and atmospheric and specialty gases are essential for various processes in wafer manufacturing.</p></p>
