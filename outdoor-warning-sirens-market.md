<p><p>Outdoor warning sirens are used to alert people about emergencies such as severe weather or other dangers. There are three main types of outdoor warning sirens: directional, rotating, and omni-directional. Directional sirens emit sound in a specific direction, making them ideal for targeting specific areas. Rotating sirens emit sound that rotates in a circular pattern, providing coverage to a wide area. Omni-directional sirens emit sound in all directions, ensuring that the alert is heard by everyone in the vicinity. Each type of siren serves a specific purpose in the outdoor warning system market.</p></p>
