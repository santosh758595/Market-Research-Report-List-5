<p><strong>The Microwave Landing System (MLS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Type I MLS market typically refers to installations where ground-based equipment is used for both precision approach and landing guidance, while Type II MLS market involves installations where an onboard receiver is used for the approach phase and switches to ground-based guidance for the landing phase. In Type I, the ground-based system provides continuous guidance throughout the entire approach, while in Type II, the onboard receiver supplements the guidance provided by the ground-based system during the approach phase.</p></p>
