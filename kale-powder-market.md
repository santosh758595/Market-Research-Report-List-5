<p><p>Kale powder is available in two main market types: air dry and freeze dry. Air dry kale powder is produced by dehydrating the kale leaves using hot air, while freeze dry kale powder is created by freezing the leaves and then removing the moisture through sublimation. Both methods result in a powdered form of kale that can be easily added to various dishes or drinks for a convenient way to incorporate the nutritional benefits of kale into one's diet.</p></p>
