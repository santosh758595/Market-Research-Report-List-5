<p><p>Automotive static seal gaskets play a crucial role in preventing leakages in various parts of a vehicle. There are three main types of gaskets in the market: Engine Block Gasket, Cylinder Head Gasket, and Auxiliary Gasket. Engine Block Gaskets are used to seal the engine block, Cylinder Head Gaskets seal the cylinder head to the engine block, and Auxiliary Gaskets are used for sealing other components such as oil pans, valve covers, and intake manifolds. Each type of gasket serves a specific function in maintaining the integrity of the vehicle's components.</p></p>
