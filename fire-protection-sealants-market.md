<p><p>Fire protection sealants are classified into two main types: Elastometric and Intumescent sealants. Elastometric sealants are designed to expand and contract with the substrate, providing a flexible seal against fire and smoke. Intumescent sealants, on the other hand, are formulated to swell when exposed to heat, forming a protective char that insulates against fire. Both types play a crucial role in preventing the spread of fire, protecting buildings and occupants from potential hazards.</p></p>
