<p><p>Air core fixed inductors are commonly classified into two main types: Multi-layer Type and Wire Wound Type. Multi-layer Type inductors are made up of multiple layers of coiled wire wound around a core, providing high inductance and compact size. Wire Wound Type inductors, on the other hand, are made by winding a single wire around a core, offering lower inductance but higher stability and reliability. Both types cater to different market needs and are widely used in various electronic applications.</p></p>
