<p><strong>The Electronic Compact Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fan</li><li>Axial Fan</li><li>Mixed Flow Fan</li><li>Cross Flow Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic compact fan market can be categorized into four main types: centrifugal fan, axial fan, mixed flow fan, and cross flow fan. Centrifugal fans create airflow by pushing air outward from the center of the fan. Axial fans draw air in parallel to the fan shaft. Mixed flow fans combine aspects of both axial and centrifugal fans. Cross flow fans move air perpendicular to the fan shaft. Each type has its own unique benefits and applications, catering to a variety of cooling and ventilation needs.</p></p>
