<p><p>High Current Ion Implanters are used in the semiconductor industry for doping wafers with ions to modify their electrical properties. There are two main types of implanters: Single Wafer Type and Batch Type. Single Wafer Type implanters process one wafer at a time, providing precise control over ion dosage and placement. Batch Type implanters, on the other hand, can process multiple wafers simultaneously, increasing throughput but sacrificing some level of precision. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
