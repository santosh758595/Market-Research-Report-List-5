<p><p>Medical dry electrodes are classified into two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable electrodes are designed for single-use applications and are typically made of lower cost materials, making them more affordable for one-time use. On the other hand, reusable electrodes are crafted from higher quality materials that allow them to be used multiple times. Reusable electrodes are more environmentally friendly and cost-effective in the long run, although they may require proper cleaning and maintenance between uses.</p></p>
