<p><p>Rheology control additives are used to adjust the flow behavior of substances, with two main types being powder and liquid. Powder rheology control additives are in solid form and are typically mixed with the substance needing adjustment. Liquid rheology control additives are in liquid form and can be easily mixed into the substance. Both types of additives help control viscosity, flow, and consistency of materials such as paints, coatings, and adhesives in various industries like construction, automotive, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
