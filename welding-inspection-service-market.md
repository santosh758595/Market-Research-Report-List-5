<p><p>Welding inspection services are essential in ensuring the quality and integrity of welds. Destructive testing involves physically testing welds to assess their strength and durability, often resulting in the destruction of the test piece. Non-destructive testing, on the other hand, allows inspection without causing damage, using methods like ultrasonic testing, radiography, and magnetic particle inspection. Both types of testing play a crucial role in identifying potential defects, ensuring compliance with industry standards, and ultimately enhancing the safety and longevity of welded structures.</p></p>
