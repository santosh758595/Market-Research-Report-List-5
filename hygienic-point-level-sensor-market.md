<p><p>The Hygienic Point Level Sensor market can be categorized into two main types: Contact Type and Non-contact Type. Contact type sensors come into direct contact with the substance being measured, providing accurate readings but also increasing the risk of contamination. Non-contact sensors, on the other hand, do not make physical contact with the substance, reducing the risk of contamination but potentially sacrificing accuracy. Each type has its advantages and disadvantages, making the choice between them dependent on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
