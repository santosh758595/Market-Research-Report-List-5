<p><p>Centralized IP Cameras are connected to a central network video recorder (NVR) where all video footage is stored. This type of system is commonly used in large-scale surveillance applications such as city-wide monitoring. Decentralized IP Cameras, on the other hand, have built-in storage capabilities and can operate independently without the need for a central NVR. These cameras are more flexible and can be easily deployed in various locations within a city for targeted surveillance purposes.</p></p>
