<p><p>The Bilirubin Blood Test Market includes two main types: transcutaneous and bench-top. Transcutaneous bilirubin testing involves using a handheld device that measures bilirubin levels through the skin. This non-invasive method is often used for quick screenings in newborns. On the other hand, bench-top bilirubin testing is done using laboratory equipment, providing more accurate results for diagnosing conditions like jaundice. Both types play a crucial role in monitoring bilirubin levels and assessing liver function in patients.</p></p>
