<p><p>Structural steel fabrication market types involve various processes to shape and assemble steel components. Metal welding involves joining pieces of metal together using heat or pressure. Metal forming molds steel into different shapes through bending, stretching, or compressing. Metal cutting uses tools to separate steel into specific sizes. Metal shearing slices steel into sheets or plates. Metal stamping uses a press to create shapes or patterns on metal. Metal rolling shapes steel into cylinders or curves. Other processes may include painting, galvanizing, or assembling components.</p></p>
