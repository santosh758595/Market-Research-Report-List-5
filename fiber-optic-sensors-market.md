<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li><li>Extrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic sensors market can be categorized into two main types- intrinsic fiber-optic sensors and extrinsic fiber-optic sensors. Intrinsic fiber-optic sensors are designed with the sensing element integrated directly into the optical fiber itself, allowing for precise measurements of various parameters. On the other hand, extrinsic fiber-optic sensors utilize an external sensing element connected to the optical fiber, offering flexibility in measuring different parameters. Both types of fiber optic sensors have unique applications and advantages in sectors such as telecommunications, oil & gas, and healthcare.</p></p>
