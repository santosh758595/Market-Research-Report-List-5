<p><p>Acrylic pressure sensitive adhesives are categorized into two types: Rubber Type and Resin Type. Rubber Type adhesives provide high initial tack and peel strength, making them suitable for applications requiring immediate adhesion. Resin Type adhesives offer excellent durability and temperature resistance, making them ideal for long-term bonding in various industries. Both types of adhesives have their specific advantages and are commonly used in industries such as automotive, construction, electronics, and healthcare.</p></p>
