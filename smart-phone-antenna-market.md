<p><p>Stamping Antenna are antennas that are manufactured using a stamping process, making them cost-effective and suitable for mass production. FPC Antenna are antennas made of flexible materials, allowing for greater design flexibility and integration into small spaces. LDS Antenna are antennas created using Laser Direct Structuring technology, enabling complex designs and high performance. LCP Antenna are antennas made of Liquid Crystal Polymer, offering high durability, thermal stability, and performance in compact devices. Each type of antenna serves different market needs based on cost, design flexibility, and performance requirements.</p></p>
