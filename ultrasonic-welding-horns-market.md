<p><p>Ultrasonic welding horns, also known as sonotrodes, are essential components in ultrasonic welding machines. These horns are used to transmit high-frequency vibrations to weld two materials together, creating a strong bond without the need for additional adhesives or heat. Ultrasonic welding horns are designed to amplify and focus the ultrasonic vibrations onto the joint area, ensuring a precise and efficient welding process.</p><p>The global Ultrasonic Welding Horns Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period.
