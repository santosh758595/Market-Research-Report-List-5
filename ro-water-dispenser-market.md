<p><p>RO Water Dispenser market is segmented into two main types: Point-of-Use (POU) RO Water Dispenser and Point-of-Entry (POE) RO Water Dispenser. POU RO Water Dispenser systems are designed to be installed at individual water sources, providing purified water directly at the point of use. On the other hand, POE RO Water Dispenser systems are installed at the entry point of water supply, ensuring all water entering the household is purified. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of consumers looking for safe and convenient drinking water solutions.</p></p>
