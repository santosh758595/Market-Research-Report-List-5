<p><p>Tertiary amine catalyst market can be segmented into various types based on the chemical structure and properties. Fatty amines, such as tallow amine, are derived from natural fats and oils. Alicyclic amines have a cyclic structure, providing unique reactivity and selectivity. Aromatic amines contain a benzene ring, increasing stability and durability. Alcohol amines, like triethanolamine, have both alcohol and amine functional groups. Ammonium salt compounds are derived from ammonia and have versatile applications in catalysis.</p></p>
