<p><ul><li>Reflective Displays</li><li>Transmissive Displays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro-display market can be categorized into two main types: reflective displays and transmissive displays. Reflective displays utilize ambient light to create an image, making them power efficient and ideal for outdoor use. On the other hand, transmissive displays use backlighting to produce images, offering higher brightness and better color reproduction for indoor environments. Both types have their own advantages and are commonly used in various applications such as augmented reality devices, head-mounted displays, and digital cameras.</p></p>
