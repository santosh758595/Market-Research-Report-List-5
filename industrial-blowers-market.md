<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Blowers</li><li>Positive Displacement Blowers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial blowers are categorized into two main types: centrifugal blowers and positive displacement blowers. Centrifugal blowers use rotating impellers to increase the velocity of air or gas, creating a high-pressure system. On the other hand, positive displacement blowers operate by trapping a certain amount of air then discharging it continuously. These blowers are commonly used in various industries such as manufacturing, HVAC, and wastewater treatment for applications requiring air or gas movement at high pressures or volumes.</p></p>
