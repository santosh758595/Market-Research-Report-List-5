<p><p>Food Shelf Life Testing Market can be classified into two types: Real Time and Accelerated Testing. Real Time testing involves monitoring the actual shelf life of a product over a specific period of time, simulating real-world conditions. Accelerated testing, on the other hand, speeds up the process by subjecting the product to harsher conditions, such as high temperatures or humidity, to predict its shelf life quicker. Both methods help manufacturers determine the expiry date and ensure product quality and safety.</p></p>
