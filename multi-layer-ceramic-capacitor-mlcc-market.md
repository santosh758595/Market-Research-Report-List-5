<p><p>Multi Layer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCC) are widely used in both high frequency and low frequency applications. High frequency MLCCs are typically used in telecommunications, data processing, and other high-speed applications where quick response times are needed. They have a high capacitance value and low ESR, making them ideal for filtering and decoupling purposes. On the other hand, low frequency MLCCs are used in power supplies, audio equipment, and other lower frequency applications where stability and reliability are key. They have lower capacitance values but higher voltage ratings.</p></p>
