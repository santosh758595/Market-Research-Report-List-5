<p><p>Nitrogen Oxide (NOx) control equipment market consists of three main types of technologies: Low NOx Burners (LNB), Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR), and Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR). LNB technology reduces NOx emissions by altering the combustion process in boilers and furnaces. SCR systems use a catalyst to convert NOx into nitrogen and water, while SNCR systems inject ammonia or urea into exhaust gases to reduce NOx emissions. Each technology offers unique advantages and is utilized depending on particular industry and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
