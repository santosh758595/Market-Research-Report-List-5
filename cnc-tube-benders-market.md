<p><p>CNC tube benders are available in two main types - Electric and Hydraulic. Electric CNC tube benders use electric motors to power the bending process, making them more energy efficient and cost-effective. Hydraulic CNC tube benders, on the other hand, use hydraulic systems to generate the force needed for bending, resulting in higher bending capacities and faster production speeds. Both types offer precision and versatility in bending various tube materials, making them popular choices in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
