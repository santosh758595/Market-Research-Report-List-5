<p><p>EMI shielding tapes are used to protect electronic devices from electromagnetic interference. The two main types of EMI shielding tapes are metal foil tapes and conductive cloth tapes. Metal foil tapes are made from thin sheets of metal such as aluminum or copper, providing excellent conductivity and durability. On the other hand, conductive cloth tapes are made from fabric materials coated with conductive substances, offering flexibility and ease of use. Both types of EMI shielding tapes play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity of electronic devices.</p></p>
