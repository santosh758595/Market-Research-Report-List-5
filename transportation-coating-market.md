<p><p>Transportation coating market types include powder, solvent-based, water-based, and pre-treatment coatings. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and then cured with heat to form a hard finish. Solvent-based coatings contain volatile organic compounds and are sprayed on surfaces before evaporating. Water-based coatings use water as a carrier for the coating material, making them environmentally friendly. Pre-treatment coatings are applied before the main coating to improve adhesion and corrosion resistance. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in the transportation industry.</p></p>
