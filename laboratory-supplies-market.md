Tools refer to smaller handheld items such as pipettes, tubes, and flasks that are used for various laboratory tasks. Instruments are more complex devices like microscopes, spectrophotometers, and centrifuges that are used for specific scientific measurements or analyses. Equipment includes larger, more specialized items like incubators, freezers, and chromatography systems that are essential for conducting experiments and research in a laboratory setting. Each of these categories plays a vital role in facilitating scientific discoveries and advancements.</p></p>
