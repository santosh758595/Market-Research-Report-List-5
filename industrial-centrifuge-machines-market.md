<p><p>Industrial centrifuge machines market is categorized into several types based on their functionalities. Sedimentation centrifuges separate particles in a liquid based on their weight. Filtering centrifuges separate solids from liquids through a filtration process. Basket centrifuges utilize a rotating basket to separate liquids from solids. Scroll screen centrifuges use a scroll mechanism to separate materials. Peeler centrifuges peel off solids from liquids. Pusher centrifuges push solids off a conveyor. Other types include decanter centrifuges, disc stack centrifuges, and tubular centrifuges.</p></p>
