<p><p>Vaccination for animal health includes different types such as live attenuated vaccination, inactivated vaccination, DNA vaccination, and other types. Live attenuated vaccination involves using a weakened form of the pathogen to stimulate the immune system. Inactivated vaccination uses killed pathogens to induce an immune response. DNA vaccination involves introducing genetic material from the pathogen into the animal to trigger an immune response. Other types of vaccines may include recombinant vaccines or subunit vaccines that target specific components of the pathogen.</p></p>
