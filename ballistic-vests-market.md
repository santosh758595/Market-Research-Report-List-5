<p><p>Ballistic vests are divided into two main types - soft ballistic vests and hard ballistic vests. Soft ballistic vests are made from flexible materials such as Kevlar or Dyneema, offering comfort and mobility while providing protection against handgun rounds. On the other hand, hard ballistic vests are composed of rigid plates such as ceramic or metal, offering higher level of protection against rifle rounds but may be less comfortable to wear for extended periods. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market for ballistic protection.</p></p>
