<p><p>Potash market types include Potassium Chloride, Potassium Nitrate, and Potassium Sulfate. Potassium Chloride, also known as Muriate of Potash, is the most commonly used potassium fertilizer. Potassium Nitrate provides both potassium and nitrogen to plants, making it a popular choice for high-value crops. Potassium Sulfate, also known as Sulphate of Potash, is preferred for chloride-sensitive crops. Each type of potash has specific advantages and uses, catering to different agricultural needs and soil conditions.</p></p>
